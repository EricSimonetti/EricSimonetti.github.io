About Me

Eric Simonetti, from Saratoga Springs, NY, a senior at SUNY Oswego. I am going for my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, with a second major in Computer Science. I am in the honors program here at SUNY Oswego, and a member of The Honors Society of Phi Kappa Phi. I also attained the rank of Eagle Scout on January 1st of 2019.

In the fall Semester of 2019 I got my first taste of what the field of software development is like. I was tasked, along with two teammates, to create a social media-esk web application with IBM's Open Liberty software, using a microservice architecture. I created the front end GUI for this application, which was its own microservice, using AngularJS.

In the Spring Semester of 2021 I had another opportunity to work with IBM, this time on a much larger scale. My class was tasked with creating a full stack web application that is meant to be used in supermarkets, where customers would make a list of items they wanted to buy and the app would lead them through the store with the most optimal route. I was on the engine team, so I was creating the backend using Java and Open Liberty. I developed the “store” microservice, which handled all requests about items to the database, and created the optimized path through the store.

Although I’m relatively new to the field of software development, my experiences have given me a wide array of technical and soft skills useful for such a position. I am an extremely hard worker, a fast learner, and I welcome any opportunity to learn and grow both personally and professionally.
